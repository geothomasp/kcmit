You are here:KC Budget 6.0 > Budget Summary

On this screen – Budget Summary

A.  Budget Summary: Left navigation button – click to open the screen view.

B.  Project Period Columns: navigation arrows will generate if the number of periods exceeds the screen capacity.

C.  Budget category expansion arrows: click on the right-facing arrow to expand the category and see details; click the next level right-facing arrow for further detail.

D.  Proposal Footer Actions:

•      Back: click to navigate to the previous screen (Budget Notes)

•      Save: Click to save the proposal

•      Complete Budget: click to mark this budget version as Complete; Same as using the toolbar> Budget Settings > Budget Status = Complete. Locks the budget from further edits.
